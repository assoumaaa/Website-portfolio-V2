## How to Write Your Own Posts

To create a new blog post, simply:

1. Create a new `.mdx` or `.md` file in the `content/blog/` directory
2. Add frontmatter at the top with metadata:
   ```markdown
   ---
   title: "Your Post Title"
   date: "2024-01-15"
   description: "A brief description of your post"
   ---
   ```
3. Write your content in Markdown
